For Prospective Students

Opportunities are available through:

  • Summer research programs
  • Fellowship-based research projects
  • Capstone, thesis, or independent study topics

If you're interested in joining the CVAIR Lab, please send an email to jana@marshall.edu with your CV, academic interests, and any relevant project or coding experience.

Passion for learning and creativity is valued more than specific technical background. Students from diverse majors and backgrounds are welcome!
